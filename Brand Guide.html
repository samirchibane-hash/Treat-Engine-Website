01 · Identity
Logo
The Treat Engine logo consists of a blue gear-and-drop icon paired with the wordmark set in Playfair Display. Always use the approved lockups — never redraw or alter them.
Color on Cream
White on Navy
White on Dark
Clear space rule: Always maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the gear icon on all four sides of the logo.
Never place the logo closer than this distance to any other element, edge, or text.
✕ Don't use low-contrast
Never place the full-color logo on a mid-tone background where it loses contrast.
✕ Don't recolor or stretch
Never recolor individual elements or distort the lockup's proportions.
✕ Don't rotate
The logo should always appear at 0° — never tilted, skewed, or on a diagonal.

05 · Voice & Tone
Brand Voice
Treat Engine speaks like a results-obsessed partner, not a vendor. Confident and specific — never hype-y or vague. We respect our audience's intelligence.
✓ Write like this
"Stop knocking. Start closing."
"20+ pre-qualified water test appointments, booked to your calendar every month."
"Don't love the leads? Full refund. You keep the leads."
"We hit 18 installs in a single month."
✕ Not like this
"Unlock your true growth potential today!"
"We leverage cutting-edge AI-powered lead generation solutions."
"The best water leads in the industry — guaranteed!"
"Take your business to the next level with our platform."
Ad Copy Framework — PASA
P
Pain — name the frustration
Call out the specific problem water treatment dealers feel every day. Be precise — not "grow your business" but "still waiting on the next knock back."
e.g. "Still cold-calling homeowners who aren't ready to buy?"
A
Agitate — show the cost
Make the pain real with a number or a consequence. This is where stats live — installs lost, time wasted, deals that slipped.
e.g. "Water treatment dealers lose thousands every month relying on door-knocking."
S
Solution — introduce the mechanism
Explain what Treat Engine does, concisely. Name the specific deliverable — not "leads" but "pre-qualified in-home water test appointments."
e.g. "We run your ads and book pre-qualified homeowners straight to your reps' calendars."
A
Action — remove the risk
Close with a low-friction CTA and — where appropriate — the 30-day guarantee to eliminate hesitation.
e.g. "Schedule a free 20-min demo. 30-day money-back guarantee."
Tone Attributes
↗ Confident, not arrogant
↗ Specific, not vague
↗ Peer-to-peer, not salesy
↗ Direct, not harsh
